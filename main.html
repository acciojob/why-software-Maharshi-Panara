Why I want to become a developer?

My motivation for becoming a software developer stems from my passion for technology and problem-solving. I have always been fascinated by how software can transform ideas into reality and improve people's lives. The ability to create applications that can be used by millions of people worldwide excites me. Additionally, the continuous learning and innovation in the tech industry inspire me to stay updated with the latest advancements. I enjoy the challenge of tackling complex problems, and software development provides an ideal platform to apply my creativity and analytical skills in meaningful ways.
